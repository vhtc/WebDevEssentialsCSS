In fields of green where moonlight gleams,

Amidst the night's enchanting dreams,

A symphony of whispers rise,

As crickets sing beneath the skies.

With legs that strum, a rhythmic hum,

Their melodies to night succumb,

A chorus woven in the dark,

A soothing balm for weary hearts.

Their tiny forms in grassy sea,

Each note a gift of mystery,

A tapestry of hidden tales,

Unveiled within their gentle scales.

They serenade the silver moon,

Their music like a precious boon,

A lullaby for stars above,

A testament to life and love.

Oh, crickets' song, a sacred art,

That stirs the soul and heals the heart,

In harmony with nature's song,

They guide us where we all belong.

So let their melodies entwine,

With every thought and every line,

For crickets' song, both sweet and deep,

Echoes in our dreams and sleep.
